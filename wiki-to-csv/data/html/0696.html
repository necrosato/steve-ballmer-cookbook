An astro pop cocktail is a layered cocktail , mixed drink or shooter so named because it resembles the Astro Pop lollipop candy brand. [1] Various recipes exist that use liquor and liqueurs to produce the drink. A version of the drink exists that is layered with red, white, and blue colors and served in a shot glass . [1] It is a popular alcoholic beverage in some drinking establishments . Astro Pop candy was originally produced by Nellson Candies. [2] Spangler Candy Company purchased the candy brand from Nellson Candies in 1987, [2] and the candy brand is now owned and produced by Leaf Brands . [3]

Varieties [ edit ]

An astro pop may be served as a cocktail, [1] mixed drink or shooter. [2] Several recipes exist. Alcoholic beverages used in the drink's preparation can include vodka , raspberry vodka, blueberry vodka, Prosecco (an Italian sparkling white wine), blue curaçao liqueur, Crème de banane and melon liqueur. [1] [2] [4] [5]

Drink mixers used in its preparation can include sweet and sour mix , simple syrup and grenadine . [1] [4] [5]

Additional ingredients and garnishes can include sugar, raspberries, lemon juice and lemon. [4] [5]

A cocktail spoon is sometimes used to aid in layer separation when preparing the drink, in which various beverages are slowly poured over the back of the spoon. [2]

A drink named Skylab Fallout, similar in appearance, was created at Pat O'Brien's in New Orleans in 1979, during the time of the Skylab descent back to Earth. [6] The Skylab Fallout was concocted with blue curaçao liqueur, apricot brandy , grain alcohol , rum and orange juice . [6] In its preparation, liquids that are the densest settle at the bottom of a glass. [6] This Pat O'Brien's creation involved the alcohol strength increasing as one drank it, with stronger alcoholic beverages existing in the lower layers of the cocktail. [6]

In 2013 at the Burke Street Pub in Winston-Salem, North Carolina , is version of the astro pop was the most popular cocktail drink ordered by patrons. [7]

Similar beverages [ edit ]

In addition to producing Astro Pop candy, Leaf Brands produces an Astro Pop soft drink in several flavors. [3]
