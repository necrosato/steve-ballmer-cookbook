A 7 and 7 is a highball cocktail, a mixed alcoholic drink containing Seagram's Seven Crown and 7 Up . [1] It is typically served with ice.

Mixture [ edit ]

The following is a typical recipe for a 7 and 7:

  • Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  • Add 1 imperial fluid ounce (28 ml) whisky .
  • Add 9 imperial fluid ounces (260 ml) 7 Up .
  • Garnish with lemon wedge (optional). [2]
