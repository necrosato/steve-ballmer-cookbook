Flirtini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The flirtini is a cocktail containing vodka , champagne and pineapple juice . [1] The flirtini is known for being seen on Sex and the City [1] and The Mighty Boosh . In The Mighty Boosh , it contained a twist of lime , but no vodka.

Richard Christy , noted heavy metal drummer and writer for The Howard Stern Show , likes flirtinis but calls it a "Viking's Testicle" to make it sound more manly. [2]

A Pink Flirtini is made with cranberry juice and raspberries .
