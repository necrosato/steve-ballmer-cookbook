This is a tracking category . It builds and maintains a list of pages primarily for the sake of the list itself. Pages are added to tracking categories through templates.
Pages are added to this category by Template:Commons category . It tracks cases where the locally defined link to Commons is not the pagename, and it's not the same as on Wikidata, or it is not on Wikidata.
