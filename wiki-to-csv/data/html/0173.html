Blue Lagoon
Cocktail
Blue Lagoon at the Mandarin Oriental Washington
Type Mixed drink
Primary alcohol by volume
  • Vodka
Served On the rocks ; poured over ice
Standard garnish lemon slice or Garnish the brim with an orange slice
Standard drinkware
Highball glass
Commonly used ingredients
  • 50 ml Vodka
  • 20 ml Blue Curacao
  • 100 ml Lemonade
  • 1 orange slice.
  • Ice cubes
Preparation pour vodka and blue curacao in a shaker with ice, shake well & strain into ice filled highball glass, top with lemonade, garnish and serve.

Blue Lagoon is a popular summer cocktail featuring blue Curaçao .

One variation adds a dash of lime cordial to the mix. Another variation with a dash of raspberry cordial or grenadine is known as a " Fruit tingle ", after the candy available in some countries (Australia).
