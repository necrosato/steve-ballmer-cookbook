Rebujito

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Rebujito is a cocktail invented in Andalusia that mixes Sherry ( Manzanilla or Fino ) and soft drinks . [1] [2] [3] Rebujito is a common drink in Andalusia. It is a typical drink of the Feria del Caballo (Jerez) and Feria de Abril .
