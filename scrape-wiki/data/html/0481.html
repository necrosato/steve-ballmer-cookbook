This is a list of bottled water brands . Bottled water is drinking water (e.g., well water , distilled water , mineral water , or spring water ) packaged in plastic , cartons, aluminum, or glass water bottles . Bottled water may be carbonated or not. Sizes range from small single serving bottles to large carboys for water coolers .
