Oatmeal Cookie (cocktail)

An Oatmeal Cookie is an alcoholic drink named for its taste, which is reportedly similar to an oatmeal cookie . It can be served both layered and mixed, depending on the taste of the bartender. Ingredients vary, but a sample recipe is equal parts Irish cream , Goldschläger , and butterscotch schnapps .

Alternate recipes include: Equal parts Irish cream , butterscotch schnapps , Jägermeister , and Kahlúa .

Or three parts Irish cream , three parts butterscotch schnapps , one part Jägermeister , and one part cinnamon schnapps .
