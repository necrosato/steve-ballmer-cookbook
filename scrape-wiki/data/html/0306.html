A cocktail is a mixed drink containing one or more distilled spirits , such as brandy , cognac , gin , rum , tequila , vodka , or sometimes other spirits. Usually one or more non-alcoholic mixers or a fermented alcohols, such as beer or wine , are mixed with the distilled spirits. The entire mixture is usually served in a glass , often along with a garnish .
