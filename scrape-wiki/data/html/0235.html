Adonis (cocktail)

Sherry and vermouth cocktail
Adonis
Cocktail
Type Mixed drink
Standard garnish orange peel or twist
Standard drinkware
Cocktail glass
Commonly used ingredients
  • 1.5 oz dry sherry
  • 1.5 oz. sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
Preparation Combine ingredients with ice in a mixing glass. Strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with an orange peel or twist. [1]

The Adonis is a sherry and vermouth based cocktail . The cocktail was created 1884 in honor of the musical Adonis running for more than 500 shows on Broadway . [1] [2]
